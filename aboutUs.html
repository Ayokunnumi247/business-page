About Us

Founded by Ayokunnumi Andu

Starshine Studio is your ultimate destination for exquisite makeup and beauty services located in the bustling city of Greater Manchester, UK. Our studio is dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty through a range of professional makeup services and luxurious beauty treatments, all delivered in a serene and welcoming environment.
At Starshine Studio, we believe that beauty is a personal journey, and our mission is to make every client feel beautiful and confident. Our team of experienced makeup artists and beauty experts is passionate about their craft, using only the finest, cruelty-free, and eco-friendly products to ensure that you look and feel your best.
